⚠️ Stay Safe Online ⚠️

Important Message

You may have clicked on a suspicious link. This page will help you understand the risks and how to stay safe.

Why Avoid Unknown Links:

  • Phishing: Attempts to steal your personal information.
  • Malware: Harmful software that can damage your device.
  • Financial Loss: Fake websites that can steal your money.

How to Stay Safe:

  • Don't click on links from unknown senders.
  • Look for "https://" in the URL for security.
  • Use antivirus software to protect your device.

If You Clicked a Suspicious Link:

  • Change your passwords immediately.
  • Run a virus scan on your device.
  • Contact your bank if you shared financial information.

Your safety is our priority. Always think before you click!
